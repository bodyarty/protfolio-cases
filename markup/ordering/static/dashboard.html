Smart Cart

SmartCart is a safe and secure way to remotely access and control your servers and network devices directly from SDP through a reservable KVM interface. Once the KVM is installed and your session is activated, you’ll be able to remotely configure or test your racked equipment as if you were physically on-site. KVMs must be reserved 24 hours in advance and are subject to availability. Session time is charged on an hourly basis at standard Remote Hands rates. For emergency KVM deployments, please contact the OSC directly.
